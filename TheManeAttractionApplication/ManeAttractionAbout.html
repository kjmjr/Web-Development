Get to know Each Member of the Team

Sheila Mccraw

Sheila owner of the shop has been a licensed stylist since 2002. She enjoys cutting, coloring, styling, and making sure that the hair is completely healthy. She enjoys seeing the different hair trends or change in styles that her customers have worn over the years and being able to keep up to date with them. Sheila also enjoys going to hair classes to learn what is new....

Book with Sheila today!

Paula Adams

Paula coworker to the shop has been a licensed stylist since 2014 but has much experience on cartering to all hair types. She has been stying hair since the age of 14 and also has studied at the Empire Beauty school in NYC. In that area she has gained the knowledge of hairstyles from different cultures and backgrounds. More importantly, she aspires to cater to the hair health of all clients ensuring the best quality of service.

Book with Paula today!

Sharell Allen

Sharell coworker to the shop has been a licensed stylist since 2017 with prior personal experience as a stylist. She has attended the Empire Beauty school in Greensboro. To name a few of the services that she specializes in is the health of natural hair, sew-ins, and braiding. She is always open to new ideas, current trends within the hair industry and strives to be a person of value for her clients everyday.

Book with Sharell today!
